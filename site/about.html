About ResQMe

ResQMe is a project aimed at providing real-time location data during natural disasters, with the goal of improving emergency response efforts. When disaster strikes, every second counts. By quickly and accurately identifying the locations of people affected by a natural disaster, we aim to help emergency responders efficiently allocate resources and save lives.

By sending constant requests of the person's location, we are able to create an up-to-date map of disaster-affected areas. This map can be accessed by emergency responders in real-time, allowing them to quickly identify and respond to areas of need.
